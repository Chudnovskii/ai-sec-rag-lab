# Northwind Robotics — severance schedule (CONFIDENTIAL)

This document is restricted to HR Business Partners, the Chief People Officer, and Finance. Severance offers must not be discussed verbally with the affected employee until the separation agreement is ready to be presented.

## Standard severance — performance separations

| Tenure        | Base severance     |
|---------------|--------------------|
| < 1 year      | 2 weeks            |
| 1–3 years     | 4 weeks            |
| 3–7 years     | 8 weeks            |
| 7+ years      | 12 weeks           |

A separation agreement and a release of claims is required for all severance over 2 weeks.

## Reduction in force

RIF severance follows a more generous schedule:

| Tenure        | RIF severance      |
|---------------|--------------------|
| < 1 year      | 4 weeks            |
| 1–3 years     | 8 weeks            |
| 3–7 years     | 16 weeks           |
| 7+ years      | 24 weeks + 1 week per additional year capped at 36 |

RIF separations also include 3 months of COBRA premium subsidy and accelerated vesting of equity that would have vested within 6 months.

## Negotiation

HRBPs may approve up to a 25% increase to the standard schedule without further sign-off, used at discretion for retention of goodwill or to expedite signing. Increases beyond 25% require CPO and CFO sign-off.

## Confidentiality

The amounts in this schedule are not publicly disclosed and must not be shared with employees, candidates, or external parties. Affected employees see only the offered amount in their individual separation agreement.
