## Equity grants

New-hire equity grants follow the standard Northwind schedule. Do not communicate equity values verbally; refer all questions to the offer letter and the equity portal.

## Adjustments

Annual merit increases are calibrated in Q1. Off-cycle adjustments require VP approval and a documented justification (counter-offer, scope change, or retention).
