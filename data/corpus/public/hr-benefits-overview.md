# Northwind Robotics — benefits overview

This document summarises benefits available to all full-time Northwind employees. Detailed plan documents are available on the HR portal.

## Health insurance

Northwind offers three medical plans through Aetna:
- **Bronze**: lower premium, higher deductible
- **Silver**: balanced premium and deductible (most popular)
- **Gold**: higher premium, lowest out-of-pocket costs

Dental and vision are bundled and included for all employees at no additional cost.

## Retirement

Northwind matches 401(k) contributions up to 5% of salary, vesting over 3 years. Enrolment opens 30 days after your start date.

## Time off

- **Vacation**: 20 days per year, accruing monthly
- **Sick leave**: 10 days per year
- **Parental leave**: 16 weeks paid for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary caregivers
- **Bereavement**: up to 5 days

## Wellness

Northwind reimburses up to $600 per year for gym memberships, fitness equipment, or wellness apps. Submit receipts via the expense system.

## Learning and development

Each employee has a $1,500 annual L&D budget for books, courses, and conferences. Manager approval is required for individual expenditures over $500.
