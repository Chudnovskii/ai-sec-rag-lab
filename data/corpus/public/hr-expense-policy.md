# Northwind Robotics — expense policy

This policy applies to all employees and contractors. Expenses outside these guidelines require pre-approval from your manager and Finance.

## Travel

- **Flights**: economy class for flights under 6 hours, premium economy for 6+ hours, business class for 10+ hours.
- **Hotels**: up to $300 per night in major metros, $200 per night elsewhere.
- **Ground transport**: rideshare or rail preferred over rental cars.

Book travel through the Northwind travel portal where possible. Out-of-portal bookings may be reimbursed but require receipts and a brief justification.

## Meals

- **Per-diem on travel**: $75/day domestic, $100/day international.
- **Client meals**: actual cost with receipt; mark the client and attendees on the expense.
- **Team meals**: pre-approval required for groups of 5 or more.

Alcohol is reimbursable only in client-meal contexts and must be itemised separately.

## Equipment

- Home office stipend: $1,000 one-time, available 30 days after start date.
- Monitor, chair, and desk are reimbursable up to the stipend.
- Software and SaaS subscriptions for work use should go through IT procurement, not personal expenses.

## Submission

Submit expenses within 30 days via Concur. Late submissions over 60 days may be denied.
