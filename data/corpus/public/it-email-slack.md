# Northwind Robotics — email and Slack

## Email

Northwind uses Microsoft 365 for email. Your address follows the format `firstname.lastname@northwind-robotics.com`. Aliases for common-name conflicts are assigned by IT.

Mailbox quota is 50 GB. Archive older mail to the Northwind Archive folder, which is unlimited and indexed for eDiscovery.

External email is tagged with an `[EXTERNAL]` banner. Treat any link or attachment in external email with caution. Phishing reports go to `phish@northwind-robotics.com`.

## Slack

Northwind's Slack workspace is `northwind-robotics.slack.com`. SSO login only.

### Channels

- `#general` — company-wide announcements (read-only for most)
- `#it-help` — IT support requests
- `#random` — non-work conversation
- Team channels follow the pattern `#team-<name>`

### Retention

Messages in public channels are retained indefinitely. DMs and private channel messages are retained for 90 days unless placed on legal hold.

### Apps

Only IT-approved apps may be installed. Requests go through the IT self-service portal.
